ReEASErve

ReEASErve is a proposed system for our thesis, designed to provide a specific platform for student and faculty members to reserve, borrow, and manage the academic resources available in the College of Science (COS) and College of Computing and Information Technology (CCIT) at Adamson University (AdU).

Problem

The manual process of managing equipment reservations was time-consuming and prone to scheduling conflicts. Lack of real-time availability tracking hindered planning for teachers, while the paper-based system provided no analytical insights, making resource allocation inefficient and increasing the risk of theft or loss.

Solution

Developed ReEASErve, an online resource reservation and borrowing system that replaced manual processes, ensuring real-time availability tracking, reducing scheduling conflicts, and providing data-driven insights for efficient resource management. Evaluated using the ISO 25010 Quality Model, the system achieved high functional suitability, scalability, and usability, earning excellent ratings from users and experts.

Tech Stack

- Laravel/PHP
- HTML/CSS/JavaScript/JQuery
- Tailwind CSS
- Chart JS
